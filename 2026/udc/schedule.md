## Program Overview

The Computational Social Science Institute brings together scholars, practitioners, and students to explore how computational methods can be applied to questions of justice, power, democracy, and identity. The Institute emphasizes interdisciplinary collaboration, ethical data use, and applied research connecting computational tools to real-world social challenges.
