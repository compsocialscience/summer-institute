# Schedule

(The schedule is subject to change; its most updated version can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1n7jwHC-f3Ua5tz_2ELHhBM1-sH-xvOXaPNqsIPwXSv8/edit?usp=sharing)).

**Time**: Monday, June 15 – Friday, June 26, 2025 (excluding Friday, July 19 – Sunday, June 21). The program runs from 9 a.m. – 5 p.m. every day.

**Location**: The main location is **Towne Building, Room 225** (also known as Raisler Lounge). It is located at 220 S. 33rd Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104\. Here is the location on Google Maps: [https://maps.app.goo.gl/zPhF5U384EWRSSrv7](https://maps.app.goo.gl/zPhF5U384EWRSSrv7). The building looks like this from the outside: [https://facilities.upenn.edu/maps/locations/towne-building](https://facilities.upenn.edu/maps/locations/towne-building).

**Important**: On Thursday, June 18, only, we will be in the **Perelman Center for Political Science and Economics, Room 200**. It is located at 133 S. 36th Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104\. Here is the Google Maps link: [https://maps.app.goo.gl/66SeWmy6m2sUuLte9](https://maps.app.goo.gl/66SeWmy6m2sUuLte9). The building looks like this: [https://facilities.upenn.edu/maps/locations/perelman-center-political-science-and-economics](https://facilities.upenn.edu/maps/locations/perelman-center-political-science-and-economics).

**How to get in**: If you are a Penn student, your PennCard should already give you access to these buildings. If you are an external visitor, please contact us via Slack or the phone so we can get the door for you. The TAs and Penn participants will be present throughout the event to help you with this.

**Travel and accommodation**: You are responsible for your own arrangements. We recommend you reach out to the other participants on Slack to set up shared accommodation if you wish to.

**Food**: Morning coffee and lunch will be provided every day of the event.

**Requirements**: You are required to (1) attend every day of the Summer Institute; (2) present your past or ongoing work; (3) work in groups toward a research project; and (4) participate in the presentation of that project, including the proposal and final presentation.

**Prizes and awards**: At the end of the Summer Institute, you will be conferred a certificate of participation. We will also give special awards to two best projects: one voted by the faculty and TAs, and one voted by your peers.
