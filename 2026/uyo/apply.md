# Apply

## About the Summer Institute

<p align='justify'> The Summer Institute in Computational Social Science (SICSS) is a globally recognized intensive training programme that bridges the world of social inquiry and data-driven analysis. SICSS Nigeria brings together scholars, researchers, and practitioners committed to harnessing the power of computational methods in the service of pressing social questions. This two-week hybrid institute is Funded by the Templeton World Charity Foundation.</p>



## Who Should Apply?

We particularly encourage applications from:
<p align='justify'>•	Postgraduate students working on socially engaged research problems</p>
<p align='justify'>•	Early-career university faculty seeking to integrate computational methods into their research</p>
<p align='justify'>•	Industry and policy professionals applying data analytics to address real-world social, economic, or governance challenges</p>
<p align='justify'>•	Interdisciplinary scholars at the intersection of culture, technology, and society</p>

## Application Frequently Asked Questions

#### Can I apply to more than one Summer Institute location?

Yes, but not at the same time. For example, you can apply to SICSS-Nigeria Uyo, and then if you are not accepted you can apply to one of the other locations if it has a later deadline. However, you cannot have an applications under review at two locations simultaneously.

#### Can I participate in SICSS-Nigeria Uyo if I have already participated in SICSS at another location?

Unfortunately, no. Every year we have more applicants than we can accept, and we would like everyone to have a chance to participate in SICSS once before we allow folks to participate twice.
